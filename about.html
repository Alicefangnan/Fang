Little About Me Hi, my name is Fang Nan. You can also call me Alice. My college is School of Visual Arts where it located in Manhattan. I am an international Graphic Designer in NYC, and a current senior year undergraduate skilled with Adobe Creative Suite (illustrator, photoshop, indesign), Microsoft Office Suite, and web design applications. Able to stretch the boundaries of web design and digital storytelling to help the client’s brands out.


Web Designer Designed the personal website for the chairman of LiWei Beauty which displays the information about herself and the company; the average visits to the website per day reached 300+ Led a team of three designers to design postcards for the company and the chairman, optimize interior design of the company, and produce a promotional video that introduces the company.


Graphic Designer Led a team of five designers to create posters, brochures, and other promotional materials for Master Kong, one of the largest instant food and beverage producer in China; produced a short promotional video for one of Master Kong’s beverage products Designed posters, packaging and labels, and promotional videos for a marketing event for a new flavor of Pepsi-Cola at the Erqi Memorial Tower in Zhengzhou, which helped sell over 190 cans of Pepsi-Cola during the event in one day Designed the promotional brochure for the Central China Film and Television Base in Zhengzhou Designed layouts and graphics of WeChat promotional articles for a mobile application that helps college students find part-time jobs, increasing followers of the WeChat account of the application by 5,000+.


Interaction Designer Assisted in scenic design and the design of scene shifting for the short film Winter Hearts, which was nominated in the New York Film Festival in 2020.Guided the design of the film director’s personal business card.
